    RSVA Holiday Meeting and Social Hour


    Join YOUR Residents of Shawmont Valley Association 

    (RSVA)

    For an end of year Membership Meeting

    and Social Hour hosted by our neighbors,

    The Schuylkill Center for Environmental Education


    Join us from 6:30-8:00pm on December 11, 2024


    - Join or renew your yearly membership with RSVA

    Membership dues are $10 for individuals 

    and $15 for a two-person household


    - Enjoy refreshments with your fellow members and neighbors


    - Meet SCEE staff and learn all about what the center is up to:

        New Arboretum Status!

        New accessible playground

        Learn about their art exhibits inside and out

        See their wonderful gift shop just in time for gift giving

        And of course, what's up with the toad crossings? 


    - PLUS we will be collecting food donations for the Roxborough Presbyterian church

    to stock their pantry just in time for the holidays!


    See you at 6:30 at the Schuylkill Center for Environmental Education

    Date: 2024-12-11

    Time: 6:30pm - 8:00pm

    Video Link: NA

    Location:

    SCEE Meeting Center
